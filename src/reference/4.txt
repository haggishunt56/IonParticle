Knight Particle<br>
<br>
The knight particle is a tricky particle to avoid. Like a knight in a game of chess, the knight particle moves in an 'L' shape and can jump over other particles and radiation.<br>
<br>
These particles move surprisingly quickly, but can sometimes be forced off the board if you stand next to the edge.<br>
<br>
Threat Level: Moderate<br>
<br>
Lowest difficulty: Intermediate
